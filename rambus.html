Leveraging LLMs for Legal Queries

I interned at Rambus as a machine learning engineer in order to create an AI solution allowing the legal team to efficiently query information from contracts and have the ability to quickly draft new ones.

About the company

Rambus is a leader in the semiconductor IP space and licensing of memory chips.

The challenge

The legal team is faced with updating solutions to several licensing contracts on a daily basis, many of which are longstanding for 10+ years. However, due to an inorganized SharePoint repository, information is often difficult to find, making it hard to renew licenses, update pertinent information, and draft new contracts for similar license agreements.

The solution

I developed an internal chatbot using Llama and integrated ChromaDB to vectorize document components. These components were transformed and chunked and later retrieved using specific tokenizers from Elasticsearch's IR system. Unfortunately cannot share more details due to Rambus NDA compliance, but I grew greatly in my technical skillset for leveraging and training LLMs in this role!
