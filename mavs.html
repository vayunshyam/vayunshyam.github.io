American Fan Marketing and Mobile App Redesign

I led a team of 7 consultants to understand American sport fan tendencies, create a digital marketing strategy tailored for FC Barcelona, and redesign their fan mobile app through interactive features and gamification. You can view the Figma redesign here!

About the client

FC Barcelona is one of the world's most renowned soccer (football) clubs based out of Barcelona, Spain. With a global following of millions of fans, their marketing teams specialize in globalizing their media offerings to cater across various types of fans.

The challenge

The American soccer fan market is dominated by European clubs such as Real Madrid and Manchester United, both of which have massive fanbases within the United States. In addition, FCB wanted to find ways to generate more interaction and app usage across different types of fans, and understand better what types of media various fan groups are likely to interact with.

The solution

We surveyed various American fans and performed case studies across European soccer teams and American sport teams to understand the fan involvement strategies they have implemented to generate retention. We then grouped these fans into persona types and scored persona types based on answers to various survey questions. Finally, we synthesized recommendations towards FCB's fan involvement strategies and digital touchpoints to generate more interaction and increase retention across American fans, and redesigned their mobile app to include successful gamification features from various European soccer clubs. We also recommended data collection strategies for FCB to integrate along with these new recommendations, in order for them to gain a better understanding of fanbase tendencies.
